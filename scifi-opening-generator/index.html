About This Project

This generator creates original sci-fi story openings and endings, drawing inspiration from the greatest works in science fiction history—novels and novellas that have won the Nebula and Hugo awards.

Each refresh brings you a new piece of compelling prose, generated using AI and cached for instant delivery. The stories explore themes of consciousness, space exploration, time travel, artificial intelligence, and the human condition.
